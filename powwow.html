Powwow


The main purpose of the app is to manage all your meetings in order to increase your productivity, and avoid losing too much time setting them up.

The way it does it is by matching your calendar with the calendar of the atendees, and purposes you different options taking both available spaces. You can also set a custom date for the event if none of the offered options fits for you.

The users calendar data is saved in a server, but taking a lot of care about privacy. No sensitive data is uploaded, only start and end dates. Powwow does not need anything more than that.
